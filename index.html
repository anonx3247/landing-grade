Features

Everything automated.
Nothing missed.

Grade handles the parts of dentistry that eat your time — so you can focus on your patients.

AI Clinical Notes

Grade listens while you work and writes structured clinical notes in real time. No more post-visit documentation. Walk out of the operatory and your chart is already done.

Automatic Insurance Verification

Coverage is verified before every appointment — no phone calls, no portals. Grade surfaces exactly what's covered and flags any issues before the patient arrives.

Automated Billing & Claims

From completed treatment to submitted claim in one step. Grade handles the coding, submission, and follow-up — so your front desk doesn't have to.

Coverage Intelligence

Grade automatically calculates what each plan covers and what the patient owes — before you even pick up a handpiece. No more billing surprises at checkout.

Patient & Family Management

Complete patient records for every individual, with family relationships and shared insurance coverage properly modeled. Find anyone instantly by name or record.

Multi-Practice Support

Manage multiple locations from one dashboard. Each practice has its own data, providers, and patients — but you see the full picture from a single login.

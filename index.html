Features

Everything your practice needs, nothing it doesn't

Purpose-built for modern dental practices. Every feature is designed around speed, clarity, and the real workflows your team uses every day.

Patient Management

Full patient records with DOB, contact info, insurance, and soft-delete. GIN-indexed full-text search finds any patient instantly.

Provider Profiles

Manage your team with NPI validation, specialty tracking, and active/inactive status. List and filter with a single API call.

Appointment Scheduling

Link appointments to patients, providers, and ADA procedure codes. Designed for fast booking with minimal friction.

Insurance Tracking

Carrier, member ID, group number, and pVerify payer codes per patient. Family subscriber and guarantor relationships built in.

Multi-Practice Support

One platform, multiple locations. Practices are first-class entities — scale from a single office to a DSO without re-architecting.

Audit & Compliance

Every stage transition is logged in the patient workflow event table. Full audit trail out of the box — no extra configuration.
